Welcome to PhysioWat

This is a web platform for sensor data storage and analysis. It allows you to upload data, process them by extracting features and visualize them through suitable graphs.


The Project

PhysioWAT (Wearable Analytics Tech) consists of developing a novel web platform to support reproducible research based on physiological sensors. This platform also offers a connection to smartphones, on which it is possible to collect data from wearable sensors and store them on a relational database. Users can upload their data and analyze the stored data online, visualize data in different graphs and save them.

The Team

We are an international group of motivated and passionate students aged 17-18 years old. We were provided with the opportunity of taking part in WebValley 2015. WebValley is the FBK summer school for dissemination of interdisciplinary research. The starting point is the philosophy of combining open source tools, FBK research products and the creativity of participants in order to solve a real problem using new technologies.

The case studies

The project focuses on the quantification of stereotypical patterns in ASD children and on the synchronization of physiological responses in different relationships. The project was commissioned and supported by the ABP-lab, UniTN, NTY and ODFLab. Other than the raw data we were given, we also collected "synthetic data" with wearable sensors.
