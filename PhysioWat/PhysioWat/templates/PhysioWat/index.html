WELCOME TO PHYSIOWAT
This is a website for sensor data storage and its analysis. Here you can upload your data from different sensors and visualize graphs that permit you a better understanding.

The project

Our project is focused on developing a novel web platform for Data Analytics of Wearable Sensors for REsearch. This platform implements also a connection to smartphones, on which it is possible to collect data from wearable sensors and store it on relational database. Researchers can upload their data and analyze the stored data online, visualize data in different graphs and save it.

The team

We are a group of motivated and determined students aged 17-18 years old of different nationalities. We were provided with the opportunity of taking part in WebValley 2015. WebValley is the FBK summer school for dissemination of interdisciplinary research. The starting point is the philosophy of combining open source tools, FBK research products and the creativity of participants in order to solve a real problem using new technologies.

The case studies

The 2015 WebValley project focuses on quantifying stereotype patterns in ASD children and on synch. of physiological responses in different relationships.
